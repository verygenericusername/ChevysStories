The story of...
pineapple and pineapple II
Its been a week, since pineapple escaped. The other pineapple is flying high after sacrificing himself. Pineapple always liked having the other pineapple as a friend, now he is gone. Pineapple loves reading books, and wanted to make his own book himself. Pineapple is now making a book about his life and in loving memory of his friend. Pineapple starting writing about his friend and how great of a friend he was. He also started writing about his life. After a few days, Pineapple finished his book. He published his book to the world. Another few days later, pineapple was watching the news. He saw his book on the news. Pineapple said: "Wow! My book is on the news?" The news now said: "I recommend this book to the public, come get it now in your local library." The news now shut off. Pineapple said: "Now my book is in libraries? Amazing!" Pineapple did more research on his book. He found out he is a best-selling author! "I did it! I did it friend!" Said pineapple. His friend looking up upon him, glad he was able to do it.
THE END
